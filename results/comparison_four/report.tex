\section{Methodology}
Zero-shot uses the pretrained TimesFM 2.5 checkpoint. LoRA loads rank-8 attention adapters trained on the same ticker. ICF (inference) uses in-context examples sampled from the training split with no leakage. Metrics are MSE/MAE on raw USD closes and directional accuracy on successive returns.
